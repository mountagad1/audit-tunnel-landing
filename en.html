Frequently asked questions

Everything you want to know

Is it really free? Is there a catch?

No catch. 100% free, no commitment, no credit card required. This initiative exists to help founders and demonstrate the value of an expert AI audit.

Who carries out the audit — a human or an AI?

Both. AI (GPT-4o + Claude) analyzes across 40+ criteria. A human expert then reviews, contextualizes, and writes the final recommendations.

My project is still being built — can I submit?

Absolutely. It's often the best time — before you invest too much in one direction. All projects are welcome at any stage.

How long until I receive the audit?

Usually within 48 to 72 business hours. AI processes immediately; the expert finalizes in the following days.

Can I opt out of public sharing?

Yes, absolutely. Each permission is independent. Your analysis remains confidential if you choose.
