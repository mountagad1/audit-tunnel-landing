Frequently asked questions

Everything you want to know

Is it really free? Is there a catch?

Aucun piège. L'audit est 100% free, sans engagement, sans carte bancaire. L'initiative existe pour aider les fondateurs à progresser et pour démontrer la valeur d'un audit expert assisté par l'IA.

Who conducts the audit — a human or AI?

Both. AI (GPT-4o + Claude) automatically analyzes your site across 40+ objective criteria. A human expert then reviews the analysis, contextualizes it to your industry, and writes the final recommendations. You get AI speed and human judgment.

My project is still in development — can I still submit?

Absolutely. In fact, it's often the best time to get an audit — before over-investing in one direction. All projects are welcome, regardless of stage.

How long until I receive my audit?

Usually within 48 to 72 business hours. AI processes your submission immediately; the human expert finalizes the report in the following days.

Can I opt out of having my site featured in a video?

Yes, absolutely. The permissions in the form are independent — you can receive the audit without authorizing public sharing. Your analysis remains confidential if you prefer.
